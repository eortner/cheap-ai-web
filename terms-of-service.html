2. Description of Service

Cheap AI for Workplace is a Jira plugin that:

  • Automatically extracts issue data (summaries, descriptions, comments, metadata) from your Jira instance
  • Recursively fetches linked issues (epics, dependencies, blockers) through Jira's linking system
  • Transforms aggregated data into structured AI prompts optimized for language models
  • Provides one-click copying of generated prompts to your clipboard
