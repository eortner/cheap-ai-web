1. Introduction

Cheap AI for Workplace ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Jira plugin and visit our website.

This policy applies to:

  • The Cheap AI for Workplace plugin installed in your Atlassian Jira instance
  • Our website at cheap-ai.net
  • Email communications with our support team

2. What Data We Collect

Jira Plugin Data (Processed Locally, Not Stored)

The plugin accesses the following Jira data temporarily in memory only to generate AI prompts:

  • Issue summaries, descriptions, and comments
  • Issue metadata (status, priority, assignee, reporter, dates)
  • Linked issue references and relationships
  • Project names and issue keys

Important: This data is processed in real-time and never transmitted to or stored on our servers. It exists only in your Jira instance's memory during prompt generation.

Website & Support Data (Collected)

  • Contact Form Submissions: Name, email address, company name, and message content
  • Email Communications: Correspondence with contactcheapai@gmail.com
  • Analytics: Basic visit data via GitHub Pages (see Section 7)

3. How We Use Your Data

Data Type Purpose Retention
Jira Issue Data Generate AI prompts within your instance Not retained (processed in real-time only)
Support Emails Provide technical assistance and bug resolution 90 days after case resolution
Contact Form Submissions Respond to sales inquiries and support requests 90 days after response

4. Data Processing & Storage

Local Processing Model

Cheap AI for Workplace operates entirely within your Jira environment:

  • Zero Data Transmission: No Jira issue data is sent to our servers
  • Zero Data Retention: We cannot access, store, or view your Jira data
  • Zero Third-Party Sharing: Your data is never shared with AI providers or other third parties

All prompt generation happens client-side within your browser and Jira instance.
